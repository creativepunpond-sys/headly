### 1. **Phone / Voice Call**

**Average Handle Time (AHT):**
- Industry Standard: **6-8 minutes** (360-480 seconds)

- E-commerce: **5-6 minutes** (300-360 seconds)

- Standard: **30-40 calls/day**
- BPO: **35-45 calls/day**

- Healthcare: **25-35 calls/day**

**Occupancy Rate:**
- Target: **80-85%**
- BPO: **85-90%**

### 2. **Live Chat (Web Chat)**

**Average Handle Time (AHT):**
- Industry Standard: **10-12 minutes**
- E-commerce: **8-10 minutes**
- B2B/SaaS: **12-15 minutes**

**Concurrent Chats:**
- Standard: **3-4 concurrent chats**
- Experienced agents: **4-5 concurrent chats**
- E-commerce: **3-4 concurrent chats**

**Chats Per Day:**
- With 3 concurrent: **35-45 chats/day**
- With 4 concurrent: **45-55 chats/day**

**Response Time:**
- First response: **<2 minutes** (target)
- Average: **42 seconds** (benchmark)

### 3. **Email Support**

**Average Handle Time (AHT):**
- Industry Standard: **10-15 minutes per ticket**
- Simple inquiries: **8-10 minutes**
- Complex issues: **15-20 minutes**

**Tickets Per Day:**
- Standard: **30-50 tickets/day**
- Simple support: **50-70 tickets/day**
- Complex support: **20-30 tickets/day**
- **Asia-Pacific Average: 40-45 tickets/day**

**Response Time:**
- First response: **<24 hours** (standard)
- Best practice: **<4 hours**
- Resolution time: **24-48 hours**

### **Shrinkage Rates**
Shrinkage = Non-productive time (breaks, training, meetings, absences)

- **BPO/Call Centers:** 18-22% (average: 19%)
- **Banking/Financial:** 12-18% (average: 15%)
- **Fintech:** 18-22% (average: 19%)
- **E-commerce:** 18-22% (average: 20%)
- **Hospitality:** 20-25% (average: 22%)
- **Healthcare:** 15-20% (average: 18%)
- **Government:** 12-18% (average: 15%)
- **Logistics:** 18-22% (average: 20%)
- **B2B:** 12-18% (average: 15%)

**Components of Shrinkage:**
- Breaks: 6-8%
- Training: 3-5%
- Meetings: 2-4%
- Absences: 3-5%
- System downtime: 1-2%

### **Occupancy Rates**
Occupancy = Productive time / Available time

- **Target Range:** 80-85%
- **BPO:** 85-90%
- **Most industries:** 80-85%
- **Government:** 75-80%

**Note:** >90% occupancy leads to agent burnout

### **Service Level**
- **Standard:** 80% of calls answered within 20 seconds (80/20)
- **Premium:** 90% of calls answered within 15 seconds (90/15)
- **Chat:** 80% of chats answered within 60 seconds

## ðŸ“Š Thailand / Asia-Pacific Specific Notes

### **Thailand Market Characteristics:**
1. **LINE is dominant:** 95% penetration
2. **Response time expectations:** Faster than Western markets (<5 min vs <15 min)
3. **Language:** Thai + English required
4. **24/7 Operations:** Common in fintech/crypto
5. **PromptPay payments:** Preferred payment method

### **Cultural Considerations:**
1. **Politeness matters:** Thai customers expect courteous, patient service
2. **Indirect communication:** Agents need training in Thai cultural norms
3. **Face-saving:** Escalations handled delicately
4. **Relationship-building:** Personal connection valued

### **Asia-Pacific Trends 2024:**
1. **AI/Chatbots:** Handling 40-60% of tier-1 inquiries
2. **Omnichannel:** Customers expect seamless channel switching
3. **Mobile-first:** 80%+ interactions from mobile devices
4. **Social messaging:** LINE, Telegram, Facebook preferred over email
5. **Video support:** Emerging in healthcare, banking KYC
